About Our Team

We are local Arctic adventure guides with deep knowledge of Kiruna and Swedish Lapland. Our team has been sharing the magic of the North with travelers for over 10 years.

We specialize in authentic experiences: Northern Lights tours, Sami culture visits, Ice Hotel tours, and winter adventures. All our guides are certified and passionate about sustainable tourism.
